Survival-based analysis

Survival curves


Kaplan-Meier estimator was initially used to estimate the survival distribution function of males and female flies. The survival probabilities of male flies are found to be higher than in females, meaning males tend to experience longer bouts as the probability of a longer bout 'surviving' in males is higher then females (see Fig. 9). Log rank analysis comparing male and female flies (p < 0.0001) showed significant diffrence in survival probability between the sexes.
